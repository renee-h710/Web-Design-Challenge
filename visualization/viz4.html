Wind Speed


The chart for wind speed shows that, the more northern the latitude, the higher the chance for more drastic wind speeds. A normal level of wind speed across the board seems to be under 10mph. In the southern hemisphere, wind speeds do not reach over 25mph. However in the northern hemisphere, speeds can get up to 35 mph. More drastic speeds indicate weather phenomenons like hurricanes and storms.
